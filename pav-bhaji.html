Pav Bhaji Recipe

Pav Bhaji is an Indian food recipe that has all vegetables smashed and is flavoured using different spices. Here we will learn how to cook Pav Bhaji using basic and home-available vegetables. Feel free to skip any veggie if you want. ( ^_^ )

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Cauliflower
  • Green Peas
  • Coriander (for garnishing)
  • Tomato
  • Carrots
  • Pav Bhaji Masala
  • Necessary Spices

Instructions

  1. Add some oil in pan / pressure cooker and sauté onion till golden brown.
  2. Then add tomatoes and sauté them for approximately 2–3 mins. Then add all available veggies like Cauliflower, Green Peas, Carrots, etc.
  3. Sauté a bit and add 1½ cups of water. Close the lid of the pan or pressure cooker and let it cook for 20 mins.
  4. In another pan, take some oil, then add ginger-garlic paste to it, then sauté some onion until golden brown.
  5. Add spices such as turmeric, salt, chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and then add the cooked mixture after smashing it into a fine paste.
  6. Stir it for a bit, add water according to your preferred thickness, and adjust salt to taste.
  7. Now your pav bhaji is ready to eat. Garnish it with some coriander and eat with delight.
  8. If possible, add some raw onion and coriander before eating. It brings out the authentic taste.
