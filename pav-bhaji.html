Pav Bhaji Recipe

Pav Bhaji is a famous Indian street food made by mashing mixed vegetables and cooking them in a spicy, buttery gravy. It is served with soft bread rolls toasted in butter — a delicious and comforting meal loved by many.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Cauliflower
  • Green Peas
  • Coriander (for garnishing)
  • Tomato
  • Carrots
  • Pav Bhaji Masala
  • Necessary Spices

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion in oil until golden brown.
  2. Add tomatoes, then mix in all chopped vegetables (cauliflower, peas, carrots, etc.).
  3. Add water and cook for about 20 minutes.
  4. Prepare a tempering with oil, onion, and ginger-garlic paste, then mix in the cooked veggies.
  5. Add spices (turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, pav bhaji masala).
  6. Smash into a smooth mixture, adjust thickness with water, and season with salt.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with pav.
